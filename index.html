About Campus360

Our Mission

Campus360 bridges the gap between students and administration by providing a seamless platform for reporting and tracking campus issues. We believe every student's voice matters in creating a better campus environment.

  • ✅ Real-time issue reporting and tracking

  • ✅ Analytics and insights for better decisions
  • ✅ Mobile-responsive design
